Egg Creation Rules

  • Required details: Every egg needs a clear name, a helpful hint, and a max redeem limit of at least 1.
  • Appropriate content: All uploaded texture images must be safe and event-appropriate.
  • Prerequisite: You must create at least one egg before you are allowed to redeem others.

Hiding & Redeeming

  • The QR Code: Submitting an egg generates a QR code and link. You can find your QR codes in your eggs tab. This is the only way hunters can redeem it.
  • Physical hiding: Print, draw, or display the QR code in a safe, accessible location. Do not litter the university or place codes in restricted/private areas.
  • Digital hiding: Share the QR image or link as part of an online clue.
  • Clear hints: Write helpful hints that guide players directly to the QR code's location, rather than just vaguely describing the area.
